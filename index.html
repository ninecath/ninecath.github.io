ascii-creator is a website made for the creation of ASCII arts, more specifically for the program nincat, which displays ASCII art randomly in the terminal.

To pick a color, you can either click on a block of the pallete at the bottom, or in your keyboard top keys (1-8), with CTRL enabled it chooses the bright colour.
